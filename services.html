» Aircraft Manufacturer Delivery Flights

Flight Planning, Logistics, Fuel, Handling, Ferry Permits; we provide a complete turnkey solution. World Air Ops is the sole external support partner for new de Havilland DHC-6-400 deliveries, and provides ad-hoc support for Airbus.

 

» Ferry Flight Management

We work directly with airlines and lessors to fully manage, or assist with, Ferry Flights for all aircraft types.

 

» Aircraft Importation

If your ferry includes a Sale and Purchase agreement, we work with Customs in advance to make all arrangements, so that there is minimal delay on arrival.

 
» Fuel Flow ® low-cost Jet-A1 fuel

Because we purchase our own fuel, and use our volume to reduce normally fixed differentials, we offer our customers our unique Fuel Flow ® discount. We supply over 1 million US Gallons annually.

 

» Special Route Flight Planning

» ATC Route and Airspace Negotiation

» Helicopter / Ship / Aircraft movements

» Airshow coordination

 We have long established relationships with ATC agencies and Airports worldwide, and our fully licensed dispatchers provide specialist Flight Planning advice. We run all plans in-house, with full support for ETOPS, ETP's, De-pressurisation, and Engine-out scenarios.
   
» "Boots on the Ground" Knowledge

We are well versed in specfic regional support, in particular South America, Africa, South Pacifc, Middle East and Asia.

 

» Diplomatic and Special Flight Permits

 We actively manage Diplomatic, Special, Ferry and regular Overflight and Landing permit requests.
